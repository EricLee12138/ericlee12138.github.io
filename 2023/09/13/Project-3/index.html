Gnome Go Home

Gnome Go Home is an AR game on Magic Leap. The gnome is the main character that just finishes his barbecue in the forest anD wants to go home. However, the smell of the barbecue attracts a lot of scary dogs which the gnome fear very much. The guest will play the game as a protector who will try to keep the dogs away from the gnome and help him get home safely.

Whale You Were Here

Whale You Were Here is a VR game on HTC Vive. The guest will drive a space ship and freely explore a fancy sea-like space. In the space, there are a lot of animals trapped by bubbles, which can be saved by the guest using a magic wand. Following the star path, the guest will poke as many bubbles as possible and finally reaches the final bubble trapping a blue whale. After saving the final blue whale, all animals that the guest saves will gather together and beautifully swim up towards the sky.

Beat Fighter

Beat Fighter is a 3D rhythm game on PC. The main character is a robot character, whose direction can be controlled by the guest using 3D Rudder and movement controlled using Phidgets. The background has a cyber punk style and will have obvious beat and rhythm, so that the robot can only move forward/backward when the guest hits the beat correctly on time.

Trapped

Trapped is a 2D horror game on CAVE, an ETC platform that consists of three huge screen and one rubber platform on the ground. The game can be played by 5-6 players at the same time and they will experience as tourists that accidentally walk in a haunted house and try to get out before being trapped forever. The game contains several puzzles, during which the guests step by step reveal the story of the house.

Tha Zubmarin

Tha Zubmarin is an immersive game experience for two players, one being in VR and diving in the deep ocean, and the other being in the actual world and using Makey Makey to control the submarine. The two players need to continuously talk and cooperate to fix the submarine and escape from the ocean.
