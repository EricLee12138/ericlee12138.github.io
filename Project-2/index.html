project

Eno's Umbrella

Unity

Game

Mobile

Overview

Duration: 1 month

Role: Programmer

Team: 2 game designers, 4 programmers, 4 artists

Platform: Unity, Mobile

Description

What can a magic umbrella do?

Open the umbrella, Eno can glide in the air.

Use the umbrella surface as a fulcrum, Eno can sway in the air.

Extend the umbrella and then contract it quickly, Eno can fly to the sky.

In this game world, there is such a magical umbrella to help Eno the Princess to go across the obstacles, resist the danger, climb the canyon.

Use your imagination and take Eno home.

Demo

The game has been published on TapTap, but is only available in China right now.
